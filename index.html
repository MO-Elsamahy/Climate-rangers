Curriculum

Training Modules

Five comprehensive modules covering all aspects of climate diplomacy and sustainable development leadership.

01

Introduction to Climate Diplomacy

Build foundational knowledge of climate science and understand the evolution of global climate agreements.

Content Modules:

  • Climate Change Fundamentals
  • Evolution of Global Climate Policy
  • Key Milestones (Rio, Kyoto, Paris)

Key Skills:

  • Climate science literacy
  • Historical contextual analysis
  • International diplomacy basics

Learning Activities:

  • Case Study: Stockholm 1972 (online discussion)
  • Virtual Group Debate
02

Global Climate Governance

Understand governance architecture and analyze roles of major international institutions.

Content Modules:

  • UNFCCC Framework
  • Multilateral Environmental Agreements
  • Role of IPCC, UNEP, WMO

Key Skills:

  • Institutional analysis
  • Global agreement assessment
  • Multilevel governance

Learning Activities:

  • Online COP Simulation
  • Debate: Paris vs. Kyoto (Zoom breakout rooms)
03

National & Regional Climate Policies

Analyze national climate actions and explore regional dynamics in climate governance.

Content Modules:

  • NDCs (Nationally Determined Contributions)
  • Regional Cooperation (EU, Africa, MENA)
  • Subnational & Local Climate Policies

Key Skills:

  • Policy evaluation
  • Regional strategy formulation
  • Local governance knowledge

Learning Activities:

  • Online Workshop: Regional Strategy Planning
  • Case Comparison: NDCs
04

Climate Negotiation Strategies

Master negotiation tactics and learn coalition-building and strategic positioning.

Content Modules:

  • Negotiation Theories
  • Multilateral Dynamics
  • Coalition Politics (G77, AOSIS, BASIC)

Key Skills:

  • Negotiation skills
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Strategic communication

Learning Activities:

  • Mock COP (online role-play)
  • AI Tools for Negotiation Prep
05

Climate Finance

Understand finance systems and design comprehensive climate finance proposals.

Content Modules:

  • Climate Finance Sources
  • Economic Instruments
  • Proposal Design & Reporting

Key Skills:

  • Financial analysis
  • Proposal writing
  • AI-assisted modeling

Learning Activities:

  • Virtual Workshop: Proposal Development
  • Case Study: EU Green Deal
